Welcome to The Full Explanation!

This website was designed to fix the problem of when you wish to understand a complicated term X, but the explanation itself has complicated terms A, B, C, D, E, F and so on.

Here, as you will notice, certain topics are highlighted in the generated explanation. Clicking those topics will generate an explanation of those topics (AB,C...) in the context of topic X.

What if the explanations of A,B,C have the same problem?
No worries, the same features still apply!

HOW TO USE

×

1. Enter a topic from any domain, preferably mathematical / scientific / historic. If you sense your topic may have more than one meaning, add additional keywords to ensure you get the explanation you desire. Once you have clicked, give it ~10 seconds to generate.

2. Once your explanation is generated, you will observe that certain words are highlighted. If one of those words are a term you don't understand, you can click it to generate an explanation of that word in the context of the topic you seek to understand.

3. If that explanation has been generated, you are good to go!
If you don't understand certain highlighted words in the new explanation, everything from step 2 still applies.

Additional Note: All new explanations of highlighted terms appear above the paragraph they were clicked from.
